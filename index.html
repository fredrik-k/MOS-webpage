The City Sound Wave Project

Celsius Projects 12 - 18

Yasmin Thomsen, Martin Bonde-Hansen, Russell Cannon, Mimmi Green

We are The City Sound Wave Project. We are exploring the intersection between the planned city and the lived city. We are currently looking into the redevelopment of Norra Sorgenfri and the values and challenges associated with it. At Malmö Open Studios we would like to have an open dialogue about people’s experiences of and expectations for the area. This will contribute towards a podcast capturing the voices of Norra Sorgenfri
